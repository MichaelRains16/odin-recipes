Shipwreck Dinner

Description:

It might not be pretty, but it sure is easy and tasty!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Cook beef for 5-7 minutes.
  2. Add ingredients. Stir and cook for about 12 minutes.
  3. Season with salt. Stir half of cheese into the mix, put the other half on top.
